    Questions customers will ask

    FAQ

    Why is it cheaper?

    SeoulShelf is positioned around Korea-direct sourcing, consolidated drop shipments, bundle-first orders, and lean U.S. fulfillment instead of inflated one-off resale pricing.

    Why does delivery take longer?

    Products are sourced in Korea, grouped into a drop, exported together, handed off in Boston, and then delivered domestically.

    Do I get free masks with my order?

    $49+ orders unlock a free 3-piece Seoul Sample Kit, usually masks, minis, or surprise skincare extras. $75+ and $100+ orders unlock upgraded bonus tiers. Bonus items are while supplies last.

    Is shipping really free?

    Yes for the customer in this prototype. The price needs to include the real cost of international movement, customs, packaging, bonus items, and U.S. delivery.

    Why are bundles emphasized?

    Bundles raise average order value, make free shipping and free samples more realistic, and help the business avoid losing money on tiny single-product orders.

    Will you sell sunscreen?

    Not in the pilot catalog. SPF and medical-claim products need extra compliance review before being sold or included as samples in the U.S.

    Can I track my order?

    Yes. Customers receive Korea-drop updates first, then final carrier tracking once the package reaches the U.S. delivery leg.
